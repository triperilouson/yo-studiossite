POLICY / COOKIES

COOKIES

Launch draft — analytics and advertising categories must be updated if those services are added.

ESSENTIAL

YO uses essential security mechanisms required for authentication, refresh sessions, cart and checkout operation. The refresh credential is stored in a secure HttpOnly cookie.

OPTIONAL

Analytics, advertising and behavioural tracking are not described as active until their providers, purposes and consent controls are configured.

CONTROL

Browser settings can remove cookies, although blocking essential storage may prevent account and checkout functions from working.
