YOCONTACT
POLICY / PRIVACY

PRIVACY

Launch draft — controller identity, retention periods and provider list must be completed before production.

DATA

YO processes account, contact, delivery, order, security and support information required to operate the store. Card details must be entered only on the hosted payment-provider interface and are not stored by YO.

PURPOSE

Data is used to authenticate accounts, fulfil orders, prevent fraud, provide support, meet legal obligations and send requested transactional communications.

SECURITY

Passwords are stored as Argon2id hashes. Administrative access, sessions and privileged actions are protected and audited. No online system can promise absolute security.

RIGHTS

Customers may contact YO about access, correction or deletion requests, subject to identity verification and records that must be retained by law.
